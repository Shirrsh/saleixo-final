# Product: Saleixo

Saleixo is a B2B ecommerce services platform targeting product sellers on marketplaces like Amazon, Flipkart, Etsy, Shopify, eBay, and Walmart across US, UK, EU, and India.

## Core Offering

Professional product photography, ecommerce design, and digital marketing services for online sellers. Key services include:
- Studio-grade product photography
- Ecommerce listing design and optimization
- Social media marketing
- Brand strategy and consulting

## Target Users

- **Public visitors**: Sellers browsing services, viewing portfolio, submitting lead inquiries
- **Admin users**: Internal team managing site content via a CMS-style admin panel

## Key Business Goals

- Convert visitors into consultation bookings (primary CTA: "Book Consultation" / WhatsApp)
- Showcase portfolio and category-specific work to build trust
- Manage all site content (hero copy, services, blog, testimonials, FAQs, team) through the admin panel without code changes

## Brand Identity

- Brand name displayed as **Saleixo** in the UI
- Color palette: deep teal (`#1a3a3a`), gold (`#d4af37`), light gray (`#f5f7fa`)
- Tone: professional, conversion-focused, trust-building
